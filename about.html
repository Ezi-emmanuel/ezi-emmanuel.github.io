    About Me

    My name is Eziuche Emmanuel, and I am an MSc in Artificial Intelligence student at the University of Essex. I am deeply passionate about the intersection of advanced machine learning and clinical practice.

    My primary focus lies in how neural networks and machine learning can be leveraged to transform healthcare. I am particularly interested in the high-stakes environment of the operating theatre, where I explore the impact of AI on anaesthetics and the streamlining of patient and surgical flow. My goal is to build intelligent systems that enhance clinical decision-making, improve patient outcomes, and optimise the efficiency of surgical environments.
