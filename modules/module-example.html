Reflection

What have I learned, and how?

At the start of the module I thought of AI mainly as “prediction from data.” Working through the search and planning topics showed me that symbolic approaches still matter for explainability and for domains where data are scarce. I learned this both from lectures and from debugging a small uninformed search implementation where my first version was inefficient—I had not understood frontier expansion properly until I traced it by hand.

The essay was the turning point for my reading skills: I used a synthesis table to compare authors, which the tutor had demonstrated in a workshop. I will reuse that technique in future modules. If I did the group work again, I would allocate a dedicated “definitions” slide deck earlier so we could rehearse timing with consistent terminology.
